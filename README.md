# Terraria Mod: The Law of the Gods

A Terraria mod inspired by Lovecraftian horror and cosmic mysteries, aiming to expand Terraria's lore by introducing a darker atmosphere, deeper storytelling, and new horrors to discover.

This mod will add a large amount of new content, alongside a structured lore that players can uncover throughout their journey.

Current planned features:

* 2 new Town NPCs
* 30 new weapons for each class
* 17 new enemies, 8 in Pre-Hardmode and 9 in Hardmode
* 3 new biomes: 1 surface biomes, 1 underground biomes, and 1 space biome
* A new Snow Temple to expand the existing biome
* 4 new bosses inspired by Lovecraft's works, 2 in Pre-Hardmode and 2 in Hardmode
* Hundreds of new items, including blocks, gadgets, lore items, and miscellaneous content
* A custom soundtrack for new structures and biomes

## Lore

Long after the fall of humanity, the world remains scarred by an ancient apocalypse. Darkness has ruled for countless generations, leaving behind only fragments of lost civilizations and forgotten knowledge.

From these remnants, a new struggle begins. Ancient secrets, mysterious powers, and forbidden discoveries reveal that the past still holds the key to the future.

Explore a world filled with cosmic horrors, forgotten civilizations, and entities inspired by Lovecraftian mythology, while uncovering the mysteries left behind by those who came before.
